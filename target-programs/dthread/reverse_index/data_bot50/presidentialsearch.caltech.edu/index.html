The Presidential Search

On October 3, 2005, Dr. Baltimore announced his plans to step down in June 2006 as Caltech's president. The process to select his successor began immediately. This site was used during the initial part of the search to solicit input from members of the Caltech community about the qualities the next president should have and potential candidates for the position. The work by the faculty presidential search committee and the trustee presidential selection committee was completed and the new president was announced to the Caltech community on Friday, May 26, 2006.

Caltech's eighth president will be Dr. Jean-Lou Chameau, the provost and vice president for academic affairs at the Georgia Institute of Technology. He will take office on or before September 1. Please see the official announcement for more information about Dr. Chameau.
