The Treasurer's Office oversees the Institute's investment, financial, and risk management activities.

The Treasurer's Office reports to the President of the university with oversight by the Investment Committee of the Board of Trustees. Assets under management include close to $2 billion of endowment, trust and other assets, including but not limited to working capital and real estate.

The investment management team and the Institute's Board of Trustee Investment Committee's goal is to provide increased support for the Institute's academic and research programs through excellence in its investment management.

The financial and the risk management and real estate teams ensure that the Institute continues to support and protect its assets broadly speaking through financial strategies and risk management, and stewardship of gift assets received from generous donors.
