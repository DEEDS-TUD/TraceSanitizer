AFTER HOURS MEDICAL CARE


 Student Health Center

The Archibald Young Student Health Center is a convenient place to go when you need medical and counseling services. Located on the Caltech campus, the Student Health Center is staffed by doctors, nurse practitioners and physician assistants.

Visit the Student Health Center whenever you need health care services. You don't need an appointment---just drop in. If you wish to see a specific doctor or nurse practitioner, please call for an appointment.
