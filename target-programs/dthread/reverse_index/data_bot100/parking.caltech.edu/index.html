Welcome to Caltech Parking web site!

All vehicles utilizing Caltech parking facilities must be registered and display a parking placard.

Whether you are a student, faculty, or staff member, one of our many visitors, or a vendor conducting business on the campus, we are glad you are here. You may have already noticed parking is a limited resource at Caltech. In order to ensure that this resource can serve as many people as efficiently as possible, we ask that you observe Institute parking regulations. Your cooperation is greatly appreciated.

The quarterly incentives to carpools are being replaced by discounted parking, and free daily passes.  Carpoolers will now receive a discounted parking fee of $30.00, to be split between participates of the carpool. In addition, each carpool will now receive 3 free daily permits per person per month at no cost.

If you have any questions please call Kristina Valenzuela at ext. 4729.
