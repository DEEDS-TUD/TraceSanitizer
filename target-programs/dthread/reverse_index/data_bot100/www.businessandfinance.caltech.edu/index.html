Business & Finance Programs:

Research Spotlight for Staff

Check back for information
on future events.

The Research Spotlight for Staff is a program for Caltech staff that works to bridge the divide between Caltech's academic and administrative sectors.  The program is based on the public Watson lecture series, but in this case faculty members present their research specifically to a staff audience during the workday.  When possible, lab tours are included.  Previous speakers have included Scott Fraser, Rosen Professor of Biology, Andrew Lange, Goldberger Professor of Physics, and Jeroen Tromp, McMillan Professor of Geophysics.
