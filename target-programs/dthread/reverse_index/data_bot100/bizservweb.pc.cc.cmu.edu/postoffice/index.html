Introduction

This has been prepared to familiarize you with the mail services offered at Carnegie Mellon by the University Postal Services. We hope it will assist you in obtaining the best possible mailing results in the most cost-effective manner.

The University Post Office is a sub-contracted station of the United States Postal Services (USPS) and provides all U.S. postal services to the campus community. The University Center post office sells stamps, money orders, envelopes, tape, and boxes for mailing packages. Additionally, the University Post Office offers UPS, Priority Express, Global Priority, Registered, Certified, Insured, and International mailing services. Fax service, copies, and passport photos are also available. Post Office (PO) Boxes are available for rent by the campus community, for both departmental and personal use.

The University Post Office does not deliver packages to the fraternities, sororities, or off-campus residents.

The more we know about your needs, the better we can address them. Please inform us in advance if you know you will have a large mailing or if you will need a special service. We urge you to use our expertise and call the Manager of Postal Services at 8-2928, or call the Mail Distribution Supervisor at 8-2929

Our Mission

The mission/vision of the Postal Services mail distribution center is to provide the most efficient mailing results in the most cost-effective manner with courteous mail service.

We will accomplish this by empowering and training our staff to work as a team with continuous improvement as our team goal.

We are here to help you and will be happy to answer questions and assist you in planning projects
