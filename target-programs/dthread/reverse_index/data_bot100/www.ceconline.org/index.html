Welcome to the Child Educational Center..."fulfilling the promise of childhood" for over 25 years

The Child Educational Center (CEC) opened its doors on September 17, 1979 to serve the growing need for the education and care of young children of working families at JPL, Caltech and the surrounding community. Our goal, both 26 years ago and today, is to facilitate the development of children and support all families, in whatever form they may take. We view all people, large and small, as engaged in their own developmental process and deserving of our respect and care.

The CEC is a non-profit, full day child care center established by and supported in part by the California Institute of Technology and its operating division, the Jet Propulsion Laboratory.

The CEC has been continuously accredited by the National Association for the Education of Young Children since 1988 and just received its 5th continuous re-accreditation in June, 2003.
