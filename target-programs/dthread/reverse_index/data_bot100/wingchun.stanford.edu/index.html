About Us

The Stanford University Wing Chun Student Association is a student organization dedicated to teaching Wing Chun Kung Fu (詠春拳). We are affiliated with the Stanford Martial Arts Program.

Wing Chun is one of the more well-known Chinese martial arts, and there are many webpages that describe it. See for example the Wikipedia entry on Wing Chun. The founder of our club, Eddie Oshins had also written some pages on Wing Chun.

If you're interested in joining, first drop by and watch. The relatively unstructured and informal nature of our classes demands dedication, patience and self-motivation from the students. This does not suit everyone. We prefer our prospective members to have some idea of what they're getting into.
