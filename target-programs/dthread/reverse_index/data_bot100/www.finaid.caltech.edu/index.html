Summer Work Study: Information and applications for 2006 Summer Work Study are available in the Financial Aid Office. If you are interested in Summer Work Study, please submit the required application as soon as possible, but no later than June 1, 2006. Your entire financial aid application must be complete by June 1, 2006 in order to be considered for Summer Work Study. If you are eligible for Summer Work Study, you may begin working on July 3, 2006. Prior to June 30, continuing students may utilize any unused academic year work study that has not been earned from their 2005-2006 award. If you are uncertain of your eligibility, please check with the Financial Aid Office.
