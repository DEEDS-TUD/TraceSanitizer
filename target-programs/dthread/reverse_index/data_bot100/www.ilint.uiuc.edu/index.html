A Message from Charles Stewart,
Interim Director of International Programs and Studies

Tomorrow's truly international university will be part of complex networks that combine faculty and academic programs and research across the globe. This campus already has joint academic programs with institutions in Europe, South East Asia and Latin America and over 180 formal bilateral relationships with universities around the world.

Tomorrow's truly international university will apply the best minds, globally, to solve the pressing problems facing our global village. This campus now has major research collaborations with institutions in dozens of countries and these are expanding every year.

Tomorrow's truly international university will attract the very best students from around the world and train its domestic students to be responsible global citizens and leaders in tomorrow's world. This campus is pledged to doubling its undergraduate international student enrollment and to delivering freshman Global Studies courses to at least half of the student body by 2007.

Tomorrow is nearly here, and the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign is preparing for it today.
