Palomar Telescope
Mount Design, 1929
An early design for the mounting of the 200-inch Palomar telescope by Russell W. Porter, January 1929. This equatorial turret -- also known as the tube mounting -- was later abandoned in favor of the horseshoe mount.
Photo above is from the Caltech Archives' PhotoNet, an online, searchable database of thousands of images.
