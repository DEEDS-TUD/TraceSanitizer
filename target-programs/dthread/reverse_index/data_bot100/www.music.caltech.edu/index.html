Performing and Creative Arts

People are pleasantly surprised to discover that Caltech, in addition to science and engineering, offers unique opportunities for the study and performance of choral and instrumental music, theatrical experience, and creative classes in the visual arts. Students can receive institute credit for their participation. The Institute and the Performing and Creative Arts Faculty have a commitment to quality in performance and teaching, and a dedication to serving fully the student who wishes to enrich and broaden his/her college experience. The Institute encourages students to see and think beyond the boundaries of their own disciplines by offering courses in music history and theory, both on campus and at nearby Occidental College, with which the Institute has a combined study plan.

View current student performances by going to the Caltech Public Events Series.
